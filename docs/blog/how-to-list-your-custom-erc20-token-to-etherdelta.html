How to List Your Custom ERC20 Token To Etherdelta

This tutorial will guide you through the process of listing a custom ERC20 token to a decentralized exchange called Etherdelta. You can watch the video tutorial bellow, or keep on reading step-by-step instructions on how to do it.

If you are looking for a way to easily mint shiny, new ERC20 tokens, you can do it using our token generator tool in less than two minutes.

What is Etherdelta?

Etherdelta is a new kind of exchange, a decentralized exchange. Decentralized exchanges offer a new way of trading where all deposits are kept in public smart contracts instead of private custodial wallet controled by exchange operator. This architecture reduces risk for traders as they can audit exchange at all times, making sure their deposits are always accessible to them.

Smart contracts that Etherdelta is running are deployed on Ethereum blockchain, which enables adding any ERC20 token to the exchange. This tutorial will guide you through the process of listing new ERC20 token, and putting it in the order book so that anyone can buy it.

Prerequisites

Before we begin make sure you have the following:

  • - Metamask browser extension installed and unlocked
  • - ERC20 tokens deployed to Ethereum mainnet and in Metamask wallet address - see Adding custom tokens to Metamask

Video Walkhrough

For step-by-step instructions, read on.

Tell Etherdelta About Your Custom Token

  • 1. navigate to https://etherdelta.com
  • 2. select Other from the tokens selection dropdown (it's on the bottom of the list)
  • 3. paste the address of your token's contract in Address field
  • 4. enter token symbol in the Name field
  • 5. optionally enter decimals in the Decimal fields
  • 6. click on Go button
  • 7. Etherdelta UI updates, and now it displays the number of tokens you have in your wallet

Deposit Tokens to Etherdelta

Now that Etherdelta knows about your tokens, you can deposit them to Etherdelta's smart contracts. Follow these steps bellow:

  • 1. in the Deposit tab enter the number of tokens you want to deposit to Etherdelta
  • 2. click on the Deposit button
  • 3. Metamask Notification popup shows, click on the Confirm button
  • 4. another Metamask Notification popup shows, click on Confirm button
  • 5. Etherdelta popup informs you that 2 Ethereum transactions have been created
  • 6. click on both transaction hashes, and wait for transactions to be included in the blockchain (mined)
  • 7. click refresh button in your browser
  • 8. Etherdelta UI updates, and now it displays the number of tokens you have on Etherdelta

Create a Sell Order

If you want to sell your tokens on Etherdelta you can do so by following steps bellow:

  • 1. click on the Sell Order tab in Buy/Sell panel
  • 2. enter the number of tokens that you want to sell in the Amount to sell field
  • 3. enter the price in ETH per one token in the Price field
  • 4. click on the Sell Order button
  • 5. Metamask Notification popup shows, click on the Sign button
  • 6. click refresh button in the browser after a few seconds
  • 7. Etherdelta UI updates, and now it displays your sell order in the Order Book
